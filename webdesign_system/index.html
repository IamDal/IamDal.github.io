General Application

These are some typical ways to apply the colors in the system:

  • Surface color are large spread colors and typically apply to the entire background of a page or container

  • Primary, Secondary and Tertiary colors are typically used for dominant foreground elements on a page eg. buttons, cards, accents and highlights

  • Primary, Secondary and Tertiary Container Colors are used for background elements such as containers, sections, menu background etc.

  • This list is not exhaustive and idealy colors should complement potential imagery.

Fonts

General Application

These fonts all offer a variety of weights and sizes as well as multiple applications. a few typical uses include:
  • Reddit Sans is great for sub headings and general body of text.
  • Didact Gothic is a good substitute for reddit Sans.
  • Paytone One is a great font for headings and stylized text, it works well with large hierarchal text.
  • Play is a stylized text, works well with headings and subheadings.

Text Styles

General Application

Text styles encompass the variety of ways we can utilize fonts, from headlines to footnotes, we use text in various fonts, size and weight.

Display Text - h1

  • Font-weight: 800
  • Font-size: 40px
  • Font-family: Paytone One

Display text are the largest texts that dominate a page or section. It can be creative, playful, rigid or cursive. it's important that display text stands out.

Headline Text

Headline text are headings for different sections or subheadings for a body of text. usally more formal and closer to the paragraphs font.

  • Font-weight: 400
  • Font-size: 30px
  • Font-family: Didact Gothic

Paragraphs

Font used for a larger body of text. It must be comfortable to read and has less playfulness in the font style and size.

  • Font-weight: 400
  • Font-size: 15px
  • Font-family: Reddit Sans

Buttons

General Applicatioin

There are four main types of buttons that were chosen that have various applications depending on the style of the webpage or users preference:

  • Elevated buttons stand out from other elements on the page and are great for call to actions such as adding a new contact.
  • Filled buttons offer contrast and are often used on forms or cards
  • Outlined buttons are not as highlighted as filled buttons but call the users attention to a secondary action.
  • Text buttons have no highlights and are used for navigating a page. they're often in a different font weight and located in a navigation bar.
